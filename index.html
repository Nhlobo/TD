Website & App Development Packages

Website Development Packages

Standard Package:

Pages: 4-8 pages. Best suited for small businesses, consultants, or non-profits. Features custom homepage and inner page design, contact form, gallery, or blog, basic SEO optimization, social media integration, and mobile responsiveness.

Premium Package:

Pages: 8-16 pages. Perfect for growing businesses, service providers, or organizations. Includes custom homepage and service/product pages, blog or portfolio section, advanced SEO setup, social media and chat integration, and a newsletter subscription form.

App Development Packages

Basic App:

Ideal for simple apps with limited features. Includes user login and registration, basic UI/UX design, simple functionality (such as form submissions or calculators), and optional push notifications.

Standard App:

Suited for apps with medium complexity. Features user profiles and login systems, advanced UI/UX design with custom features, integration with third-party APIs, in-app messaging or notifications, and an admin dashboard for content management.

Premium App:

Designed for complex apps with custom features. Includes complete custom app design and development, multiple integrations (such as payment gateways or cloud storage), real-time features (e.g., chat or live tracking), advanced user roles, permissions, and an admin control panel with analytics and reports.
